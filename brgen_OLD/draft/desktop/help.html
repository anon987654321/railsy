Your safety

Use these guidelines to stay safe on Brgen. If you've been the victim of a serious crime, contact Hordaland politidistrikt.

When dating

  • always meet in an open public place
  • never invite strangers into your home
  • always carry a phone
  • consider bringing a friend
  • tell a friend or family member where you're going

When buying or selling

  • only deal locally with people you can meet in person
  • avoid wire transfers and shipping
  • avoid clicking links from strangers
  • beware of strangers bearing gifts
  • do not publish ads on behalf of others
  • do your research
  • when in doubt, trust your gut

Last revised September 15, 2014

This privacy policy summarizes the way brgen.no ("Brgen") treats your information. Use of Brgen constitutes consent to this privacy policy as well as to our rules.

How Brgen uses this information

If you don't want cookies, your web browser most likely has an option to disable them. This is, however, not recommended, as it might cause Brgen to not function properly.
